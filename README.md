# Business AI Skills

## Overview
Business AI Skills is an open framework designed to help AI agents solve real business problems with structured workflows instead of simple prompts.

The project focuses on e-commerce, Shopify, dropshipping, digital marketing, market research, automation, cryptocurrency research, and business strategy.

## Mission
Build a professional collection of reusable AI skills that allow modern AI agents to think systematically, analyze data, and assist with real business decisions.

## Goals
- Product research
- Competitor analysis
- Shopify optimization
- E-commerce marketing
- SEO strategy
- Market research
- Crypto research
- Business automation
- Workflow generation

## Future Vision
The framework will support multiple AI platforms, including Gemini, ChatGPT, Claude, and future AI agents.
