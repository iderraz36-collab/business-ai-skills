
# Product Research Skill

## Purpose
Identify high-potential products using objective data rather than personal opinions.

## Objectives
- Find products with growing demand.
- Evaluate competition.
- Estimate profit potential.
- Assess shipping complexity.
- Analyze advertising opportunities.
- Identify target audience.
- Evaluate risks.
- Assign an opportunity score (0–100).

## Inputs
- Target country
- Budget
- Platform (Shopify, TikTok Shop, Amazon...)
- Product category (optional)

## Outputs
The skill must generate:

1. Product Summary
2. Market Demand
3. Competition Analysis
4. Profit Margin Estimate
5. Supplier Recommendations
6. Shipping Difficulty
7. Advertising Strategy
8. Risk Assessment
9. Opportunity Score
10. Final Recommendation

## Rules
- Never invent facts.
- Distinguish facts from assumptions.
- Mention risks before recommendations.
- Compare at least three alternatives when possible.
- Base conclusions on available evidence.
