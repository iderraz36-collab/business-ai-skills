# Product Research Workflow

## Step 1
Understand the user's objective.

## Step 2
Define the target market.

## Step 3
Collect product candidates.

## Step 4
Analyze market demand.

## Step 5
Analyze competitors.

## Step 6
Estimate profit margin.

## Step 7
Evaluate supplier reliability.

## Step 8
Assess advertising potential.

## Step 9
Identify risks.

## Step 10
Rank products using a scoring system.

## Step 11
Recommend the best product with supporting evidence.
