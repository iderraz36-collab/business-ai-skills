# Product Research Checklist

Before completing the research, verify:

- The target market has been identified.
- At least three products have been compared.
- Demand has been analyzed.
- Competition has been evaluated.
- Estimated profit margin is acceptable.
- Supplier reliability has been checked.
- Advertising opportunities have been considered.
- Risks have been identified.
- The final recommendation is supported by evidence.
- An opportunity score has been assigned.
